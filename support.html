❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I deposit Bitcoin?▾
Go to Deposit in the navigation. You'll see a unique Bitcoin address. Send any amount of BTC to that address. Deposits are credited after 1 confirmation (~10 min). Minimum deposit: 0.001 BTC.
How fast are withdrawals processed?▾
Most withdrawals are processed in under 60 seconds. During peak times it may take up to 10 minutes. Large withdrawals over 1 BTC may require manual review.
What is Provably Fair and how do I verify a game?▾
Every game outcome is determined by server seed + client seed + nonce using SHA-256. Click the 🔐 icon in any game to access verification tools.
Do I need to verify my identity (KYC)?▾
No KYC required for withdrawals under 1 BTC/month. For larger amounts we may request basic verification to comply with AML regulations.
How does the VIP program work?▾
VIP level is calculated automatically based on total wagered. Bronze (0.01 BTC, 1% cashback) → Silver (0.05) → Gold (0.20) → Platinum (0.50) → Diamond (1.00 BTC, 8% cashback). See VIP page.
How do bonuses and wagering requirements work?▾
Deposit bonuses have a 30x wagering requirement. Must be completed within 30 days. Only one bonus active at a time.
How does the Crash game work?▾
Place a bet, multiplier rises from 1.00x. Cash out before it crashes to win. Use Auto Cashout to exit automatically at your target multiplier.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?▾
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email and you'll receive a reset link within 2 minutes. Check spam folder too.
How can I set limits or self-exclude?▾
In Account Settings set daily/weekly/monthly deposit limits. For self-exclusion email support@cryptokasino.io with subject "Self-Exclusion Request".
