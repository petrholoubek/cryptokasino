Gambling Involves Risk

Gambling is entertainment — not a way to make money. The house always has an edge. Never gamble with money you cannot afford to lose, and never chase your losses. If gambling stops being fun, it's time to stop.

✅Safe Gambling Guidelines
  • Set a budget before you play and stick to it regardless of outcomes
  • Treat gambling as entertainment, not a source of income
  • Never gamble when emotional, stressed, or under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Take regular breaks — set a time limit for each session
  • Never chase losses — accept that losing is part of gambling
  • Keep gambling separate from your personal and work life
  • Only play with disposable income — never with rent, bills, or savings
  • Be honest with yourself about how much time and money you spend
